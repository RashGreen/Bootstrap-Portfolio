Scripts

This space will contain all of the teleplays I've authored over the years. I love to create stories that play out over several arcs. Highkers is the story of two friends sharing their weekly escapades with one another. They follow a strict set of rules for relaying their stories and there are consequeces when the rules are broken. Deicide looks at what earthlings do when they find a Civilization developing on a planet Earth intends to colonize. Do we help the burgeoning civilizations or do we enslave them? Deicide is about what happens when humans are given the chance to be GODS. Green Rush follows 3 friends in the 1970s as they venture into the hidden world of growing pot. When One of the triumvriate seemingly dissapears, two friends take up a search that places them in the crosshairs of a drug cartel and a seedy gold mine owning family with a tight grip on 1970's Humboldt County.

Web Projects

Here is the space where I'll place all of my upcoming web projects. I plan on creating websites that will help politicians and voters connect in more meaningful ways. I think that the websites should be interactive while allowing for the user to imput as much or as little information as they want. I also plan to create a web application that will act as an 'online concierge'. The service will be an parallel service to websites like AirB&B and VRBO. There will also be websites to help people world build, for the purposes of writing fiction or creating games etc. The webapp will allow for the user to input various values that will impact the type of world they create. i.e. if the USER decides that the planet in their story is not in the goldilocks zone of its respective solar system, the app would not allow said plant to be a world of hot deserts. The app will allow for users to design a planet from the Macro (Solar System Placement) to the Micro (Civilizations inhabiting the planet) in order to help people create rich an vibrant imaginary worlds!
